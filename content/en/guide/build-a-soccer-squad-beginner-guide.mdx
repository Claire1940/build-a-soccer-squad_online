<Section icon={Target} title="Squad Optimization and OVR Progression">

The most critical metric in the game is your **Squad OVR**. This rating determines which modes you can access and how you rank on the global "Top 3 Best Teams" podium. Progressing through the early game requires a disciplined approach to player acquisition. You should not simply aim for "famous" players; instead, focus on filling the specific gaps in your 4-3-3 lineup with the highest possible ratings available to you.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Fill the Starting XI">
    Your first priority is to have a player in every position. A 4-3-3 formation requires a Goalkeeper, four Defenders, three Midfielders, and three Attackers. Empty slots severely penalize your OVR.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Target 2026 Squad Completion">
    Check the Captains menu. Completing an entire 2026 squad (e.g., France 2026 or Brazil 2026) rewards you with a **99 OVR Captain**. This is the fastest way for beginners to secure a high-tier anchor for their team.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Monitor the Hourly Shop">
    The Hourly Shop rotates **Prime Players** who often boast ratings of 100+. Use your saved currency to buy these directly rather than gambling on random rolls if you have a specific position of weakness.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Push for the 93 OVR Gate">
    Once your team reaches an average rating of 93, you unlock **Cup Mode**. This is where the "real" game begins, as it allows you to farm the highest-rated cards in the database.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="info" title="The Bench Fallacy">
Many beginners waste resources upgrading their bench. In the current Beta build, your **active Starting XI** is the primary driver for tournament eligibility. Do not spend rerolls on bench players until your main squad is at least 95+ OVR.
</Callout>
