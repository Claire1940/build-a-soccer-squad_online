### Squad Completion Strategy

Unlocking these captains is a systematic process. Do not try to collect multiple squads at once. Focus on one nation or club team, complete it, claim your Captain, and then move to the next target.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Select a Target Team">
    Open your collection book and choose one 2026 squad or 2002 historical team. Check which players you already own and write down the missing positions.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Monitor the Daily Shop">
    Check the Daily Shop regularly. If a missing player from your target team appears, purchase them immediately using your in-game currency.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Deploy Rerolls Wisely">
    Use your saved player Rerolls on position-specific packs to target the remaining missing pieces of your collection.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Claim and Slot Your Captain">
    Once the collection is 100% complete, claim your 99 OVR Captain or Premium Captain and immediately slot them into your Starting XI.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Unlock Benefit">
Purchasing or completing a quest for a specific player permanently unlocks that character to be rolled as a rare player variant during standard packs! This is highly beneficial for late-game min-maxing.
</Callout>
