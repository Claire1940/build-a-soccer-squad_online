<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Advanced Resource Strategy: Rerolls vs. Refreshes">

Understanding the difference between a Reroll and a Refresh is critical for long-term success. A **build a soccer squad free reroll** changes the attributes or variant of a card you already have or are about to pull. A Refresh, however, changes the *selection* of cards available to you in the shop or a specific pack.

**When to use a Reroll:**
- You have a high-OVR player but want a "Rare Variant" version.
- You are pulling from a guaranteed "90+ OVR" pack and want to fish for a 100+ Prime.
- You need to change a player's secondary position to fit your 4-3-3 formation.

**When to use a Refresh:**
- The Hourly Shop contains no players above 90 OVR.
- The Daily Shop is full of players from a squad you have already completed.
- You are looking for a specific Captain-linked player to finish a collection.

| Strategy | Resource Used | Outcome | Risk Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Variant Hunting** | Reroll | Higher stat card | Medium |
| **Shop Cycling** | Refresh | New player options | Low |
| **Bulk Pulling** | Coins + Reroll | Rapid OVR growth | High |
