<Section icon={Target} title="Reaching the 93 OVR Cup Mode Entry Gate">

Entering Cup Mode is the primary goal for any serious manager in the **build a soccer squad major update**. However, the 93 OVR requirement serves as a significant progression wall. To reach this, you cannot simply collect high-rated players randomly; you must optimize your Starting XI (typically a 4-3-3 formation) to ensure every slot contributes to the total rating. Bench players do not count toward this specific entry requirement, so all resources should be funneled into your primary starters.

The fastest way to boost OVR is to target specific "gaps" in your lineup. If you have a 95 OVR striker but a 70 OVR left-back, your total rating will suffer immensely. Use the Daily Shop to target specific positions rather than rolling blindly. The update has made this easier by introducing more frequent refreshes through quest completion.

  <Step num="3" title="Target Captain Rewards">

  <Step num="4" title="Monitor the Hourly Shop">
    Check every hour for Prime Players. Replacing a 90 OVR starter with a 100+ OVR Prime player is often enough to push a team over the 93 OVR threshold.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Resource Management: Rerolls and Refreshes">

In the **build a soccer squad major update**, resources like Rerolls and Refreshes are the lifeblood of your progression. Without them, you are at the mercy of basic RNG. The update has integrated these resources into the Quest system, meaning active players will always have a steady supply. Refreshes should be used primarily when you are 1-2 players away from completing a squad. Using a refresh when you need 10 players for a team is a waste of resources. Rerolls, on the other hand, should be reserved for "Rare Variant" hunting once you have already secured a stable 93+ OVR team.
